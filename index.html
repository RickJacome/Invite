Flight Options

Depending on how many days you are planning on staying in the area, multiple airports are available nearby that could be cheaper options. The following airports include Mcallen (MFE), Harligen (HRL) and Brownsville (BRO). MFE is the closest airport within 20 min. HRL is the second closest around 1 hr, and BRO is the farthest around 2 hr. BRO is located near the beach, waterparks and SpaceX if interested. It would be recommended to arrrive to Mcallen on Friday Dec 16 at any time of your convenience stay in a hotel in Mcallen (there are a lot of hotels near the airport). Then, the following day on Saturday, stay in Mexico, and finally leave back to Mcallen either Sunday or Monday.
