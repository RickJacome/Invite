Location & Transportation

The event will take place in Reynosa Tamaulipas, Mexico, in the Holiday Inn Reynosa Zona Dorada, located around 20 minutes from the Mcallen International Airport MFE. The hotel will have arrangements to give discount for the guests and get a cheaper hotel rates (please reach out to me for promotion code). The hotel information can be found here , and also here. For those who are flying in, a travel arrangement is being prepared for guests to take them from Mcallen, TX to the Hotel and viceversa (more details on this will be coming up). If you are interested in this please reach out to me to consider you. Also, a travel arrangement will be provided from the Hotel to the wedding ceremony (from 7-8 pm), and bring guests back for the wedding reception (starting around 9 pm).

Reynosa Zona Dorada Calle Emilio Portes Gil : 703 Col. Prado Sur

Flight Options

Depending on how many days you are planning on staying in the area, multiple airports are available nearby that could be cheaper options. The following airports include Mcallen (MFE), Harligen (HRL) and Brownsville (BRO). MFE is the closest airport within 20 min. HRL is the second closest around 1 hr, and BRO is the farthest around 2 hr. BRO is located near the beach, waterparks and SpaceX if interested. It would be recommended to arrrive to Mcallen on Friday Dec 16 stay in a hotel in Mcallen (there are a lot of hotels near the airport), the following day on Saturday, stay in Mexico, and finally leaving back to Mcallen either Sunday or Monday.
