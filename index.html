Location

The event will take place in Reynosa Tamaulipas, Mexico, in the Holiday Inn Reynosa Zona Dorada, located around 20 minutes from the Mcallen International Airport MFE The hotel will have arrangements to give discount for the guests and get a cheaper hotel rates (please reach out to me for promotion code). The hotel information can be found here.

Reynosa Zona Dorada Calle Emilio Portes Gil : 703 Col. Prado Sur

Flight Options

Depending on how many days you are planning on staying in the area, multiple airports are available nearby that could be cheaper options. The following airports include Mcallen (MFE), Harligen (HRL) and Brownsville (BRO). MFE is the closest airport within 20 min. HRL is the second closest around 1 hr, and BRO is the farthest around 2 hr. BRO is located near the beach, waterparks and SpaceX if interested.
