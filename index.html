Hotel and Location

The event will take place in Reynosa Tamaulipas Mexico, in the Holiday Inn Reynosa, located around 20 minutes from the Mcallen International Airport MFE The hotel will have arrangements to give discount to invited people for cheap hotel rates. The hotel information can be found

Flight Options

Depending on how many days you are planning on staying in the area. Multiple airports are available nearby that could be cheaper options. The following airports include Mcallen (MFE), Harligen (HRL) and Brownsville (BRO). MFE is the closest airport with 20 min. HRL is the second closest around 1 hr, and BRO is the farthest around 2 hr. BRO is located near the beach, waterparks and SpaceX if interested.
